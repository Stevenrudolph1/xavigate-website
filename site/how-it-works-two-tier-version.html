How the Library Is Organized

The library is organized around one signal and three causes:

The Signal

Renergence™ — When something that once worked becomes quietly expensive to maintain

The Causes

Structure — Load-bearing architecture (Heroes Not Required)
When outcomes depend on someone being exceptional instead of design being adequate

Alignment — Your nature vs. what you're in (Multiple Natures)
When you are competent at what you do, but drained by it—because the arrangement fights your wiring

Positioning — Where you attend from (The Engagement Map)
When you keep responding before you've actually seen what's happening

Each includes an entry book and deeper material. Start with what you recognize. Then diagnose precisely.
