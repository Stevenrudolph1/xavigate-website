What is the Move Catalog?

The Move Catalog is the complete, indexed collection of structural moves selected by diagnosis, not exploration. After your diagnostic identifies the pattern, the catalog routes you to the specific interventions that address it—57 moves organized by pattern and move family. You don't browse the catalog. Your diagnostic routes you to your section.
