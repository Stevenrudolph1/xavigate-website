Accessibility

Renergence is committed to making its website accessible to everyone, including people who use assistive technology. We follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standard.

What we do

This site is built with semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, visible focus indicators, screen reader support, and sufficient color contrast throughout.

Something not working?

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this site, please contact us at support@renergence.com. We take accessibility seriously and will address the issue.
