The Renergence™ Framework

What happens when you stop looking at one axis and see the whole terrain?

Renergence™
Framework
Structure   Alignment   Positioning
Steven Rudolph

Each of the four books above addresses one dimension of systemic failure. The Renergence Framework is the integration book—it maps how Structure, Alignment, and Positioning interact as axes in a single diagnostic terrain.

Renergence = Structure + Alignment + Positioning. When a situation returns more than it takes, all three axes are working. When it doesn't, at least one has failed—and single-lens thinking will misdiagnose which one.

This book doesn't repeat what the gateway books cover. It shows what becomes visible only when you stop privileging one lens and see the full topography.
