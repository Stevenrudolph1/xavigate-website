Licensing

If you want to teach this work to others, embed it in your offerings, or deliver it professionally—you need a license. Buying the books gives you the frameworks. A license gives you authority to act.

What Needs a License

  • Teaching Multiple Natures to students, clients, or groups
  • Delivering diagnostics (MNTEST, Heroic Load Audit) to clients
  • Building training programs using these frameworks
  • Embedding IP into courses, software, or platforms you sell

What You Don't Need a License For

You're free to use the frameworks without a license if you're:

  • Reading the books for yourself
  • Using frameworks in your own personal work
  • Recommending books to clients or colleagues
  • Loading material into AI tools for personal use

A license is only required when you teach, deliver diagnostics, or build commercial offerings using this work.
