About

Steven Rudolph

Steven Rudolph is the author of Renergence™, Heroes Not Required, The Engagement Map, and Multiple Natures®—frameworks for understanding how people and systems relate, developed over 30 years of work with over 300,000 people.

He has served as a consultant to Apple, Microsoft, Intel, HP, the World Bank, and UNDP.

He spent 21 years in India creating a school and developing the Multiple Natures framework. He now lives in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, where he runs Multiple Natures International and Renergence International.

His work focuses on making structure visible and helping people notice when systems take more than they return.

The Work

Four areas—Renergence, Structure, Alignment, and Positioning—published as books and practitioner tools. Each starts with a free ebook and goes deeper with paid material and professional instruments.

The business model focuses on one-to-many products—books, courses, tools, platforms—rather than consulting or implementation services.
