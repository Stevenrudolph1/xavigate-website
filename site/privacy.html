1. Who We Are

Renergence is operated by Steven Rudolph. We publish books, courses, diagnostic tools, and practitioner resources across four frameworks: Renergence™, Structure (Heroes Not Required), Alignment (Multiple Natures®), and Positioning (The Engagement Map).

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Steven Rudolph is the data controller. For inquiries regarding your personal data, contact us at legal@renergence.com.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

If you authenticate through a third-party provider (such as Google), we receive limited profile information (name, email, profile picture) as authorized by you during the authentication flow. We access only the permissions you explicitly grant.

For specific AI-powered tools that analyze behavioral metadata (such as the Mirror Engine), additional data access is described in the tool-specific privacy notice provided at the time of connection. Such tools require your explicit, separate consent before any data is accessed.

2.4 What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect sensitive personal data (racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, sexual orientation, biometric data) unless you voluntarily include it in free-text inputs to our AI tools.
  • We do not collect financial account numbers or credit card details. Payment processing is handled entirely by our third-party payment processor.
  • We do not purchase data about you from data brokers or other third-party sources.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Deliver products and services. Fulfill purchases, provide access to digital products, generate AI diagnostic outputs, and manage your account.
  • Process payments. Coordinate with our payment processor to complete transactions.
  • Communicate with you. Send purchase confirmations, respond to support requests, and provide important service updates.
  • Send marketing communications. If you have opted in to our mailing list or Substack, send newsletters and updates about our work. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Improve our services. Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve our website, products, and tools.
  • Ensure security. Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations. Respond to lawful requests and meet regulatory requirements.
  • Manage licensing. Administer practitioner licenses, verify compliance, and communicate licensing-related matters.

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without human oversight.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance. Processing necessary to deliver products you have purchased or services you have requested (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interests. Processing necessary for our legitimate interests, such as improving our services, ensuring security, and preventing fraud, where these interests are not overridden by your rights (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Consent. Processing based on your explicit consent, such as subscribing to our newsletter or connecting third-party accounts to our AI tools. You may withdraw consent at any time (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).
  • Legal obligation. Processing necessary to comply with applicable law (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).

5. AI-Powered Tools and Your Data

5.1 What Data AI Tools Process

When you use our AI-powered diagnostic tools, the text descriptions and other inputs you provide are processed to generate diagnostic outputs. Your inputs may be temporarily stored to deliver the output and may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form to improve the quality of our tools.

5.2 How AI Outputs Are Handled

Diagnostic outputs (reports, assessments, recommendations) are generated for you and stored so you can access them again. Outputs are not shared with other users or made publicly accessible.

5.3 Third-Party AI Providers

Our AI tools may use third-party AI providers (such as Anthropic’s Claude) to process your inputs and generate outputs. When this occurs, your inputs are sent to the provider solely for the purpose of generating the output. We select providers that commit to not using your data to train their models. We do not send your name, email, or other identifying information to AI providers unless it is part of the input you provide.

5.4 Behavioral Metadata Analysis

Certain tools (such as the Mirror Engine) may, with your explicit consent, access metadata from third-party accounts (e.g., Google Workspace). When you connect such an account:

  • We access only the metadata categories described in the tool-specific consent screen.
  • We do not read the content of your emails, calendar event details, or file attachments.
  • You can disconnect and delete all associated data at any time.
  • A separate, tool-specific privacy notice is provided at the time of connection.

5.5 AI Training Prohibition

We do not use your personal data or diagnostic inputs to train, fine-tune, or improve AI or machine learning models. Your inputs are processed only to generate your requested outputs.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 What Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies. Required for the website to function (e.g., session management, authentication). These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies. Remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience.
  • Analytics cookies. Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. These collect aggregate, anonymized data.

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not share cookie data with advertisers.

6.2 Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

For users in the EEA: we will obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies, in accordance with the ePrivacy Directive.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment processors. To process your transactions. Payment processors receive only the data necessary to complete the payment and are governed by their own privacy policies.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers. Our website and services are hosted on third-party infrastructure (DigitalOcean). These providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • AI service providers. As described in Section 5.3, limited data may be sent to AI providers to generate diagnostic outputs.
  • Email service providers. If you subscribe to our mailing list or Substack, your email address is shared with the relevant platform to deliver communications.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business transfers. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

8. International Data Transfers

Renergence operates from the United States and Cambodia. Our infrastructure is hosted in the United States. If you access our services from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

For transfers of personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to countries that have not received an adequacy decision from the European Commission, we rely on:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, where applicable with our service providers.
  • Your explicit consent, where you have been informed of the risks of such transfers.

You may request a copy of the applicable transfer safeguards by contacting legal@renergence.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period after account closure for legal and administrative purposes.
  • Purchase records: Retained for up to seven (7) years to comply with tax and accounting obligations.
  • AI diagnostic inputs and outputs: Retained for as long as your account is active so you can access your history. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Communication records: Support emails and correspondence retained for up to three (3) years.
  • Server logs: IP addresses and access logs retained for up to ninety (90) days for security purposes.
  • Marketing data: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Encrypted storage for authentication credentials and sensitive data.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel.
  • Rate limiting on API endpoints to prevent abuse.
  • Regular security reviews and updates.

No system is perfectly secure. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority in accordance with applicable law.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

11.1 Rights Under GDPR (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

  • Right of access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification. Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure. Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”), subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to restriction. Request that we limit our processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability. Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object. Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.
  • Right to withdraw consent. Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to lodge a complaint. File a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

11.2 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

  • Right to know. Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected.
  • Right to delete. Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

11.3 Rights Under DPDP Act (India)

  • Right to access. Obtain a summary of your personal data and processing activities.
  • Right to correction and erasure. Request correction of inaccurate data or erasure of data no longer necessary.
  • Right to grievance redressal. Contact us to raise any privacy-related grievances.
  • Right to nominate. Nominate a person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity.

11.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at legal@renergence.com. We will respond within thirty (30) days, or within the time period required by applicable law. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

For users with accounts on our AI tools: many rights (access, deletion, data export) can be exercised directly through your account settings.

12. Children’s Privacy

If we discover that we have collected personal data from a child under 16, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 16, please contact us at legal@renergence.com.
