Professional Application of Multiple Natures

Steven Rudolph

How do you use engagement fit as a diagnostic lens with clients—without reducing people to types?

This is the professional application guide for coaches, consultants, and practitioners who want to integrate Multiple Natures into their practice. It covers diagnostic use of the framework, client conversation structures, integration with existing methodologies, and the boundaries of what MN can and cannot do in professional settings.

The guide addresses the practitioner's own engagement patterns alongside their client work—because how you naturally engage shapes what you can see and what you miss.
