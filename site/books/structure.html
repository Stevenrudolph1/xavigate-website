Structure

Is something missing in how the work is set up?

Sometimes that means one person is carrying everything. Sometimes it means nobody is—and things just keep breaking. Structure is about how load, decisions, and boundaries are arranged. When the arrangement itself is generating the problem, fixing people won't help. The books and tools below address what's missing in the setup.
