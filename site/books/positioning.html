Positioning

Where are you attending from inside the situation?

You might be locked into fixing before you've seen what's actually there. Or holding back when the moment needs direction. Or watching from a distance that's no longer useful. Positioning is about where you stand inside a situation—and most people have settled into one stance without noticing. What you can see depends on where you attend from. The books and tools below map what shifts when you move.
