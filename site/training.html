Can this person see structurally?

Course 5: Advanced — Multi-Domain Integration

Can this person work with complex, multi-domain situations?

Where real diagnostic work happens. Complex situations rarely live in one domain. You learn the S/A/P dependency model — how Structure conditions Alignment conditions Positioning — and the sequencing logic for which domain to address first. Differential diagnosis, full Renergence audits, and the limits of structural seeing.

What You Learn

  • S/A/P dependency model and sequencing logic
  • Differential diagnosis — same surface, different structural cause
  • Full Renergence audit (detect → route → audit → intervene → self-critique)
  • Framework limits — when to refer, when to step back

What You Do

  • 2 modules, ~25-30 hours
  • 13 exercises (multi-domain detection, sequencing, differential diagnosis)
  • 8 fully worked case walkthroughs
  • 3 case submissions with expert review
