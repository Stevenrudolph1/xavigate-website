What You Get

The books lay out the full framework. The training is where you practice using it — on real situations, with structured feedback. You apply structural seeing, submit your analysis, and find out where your seeing is sharp and where it drifts back to personal narrative.

What the training includes

  • Application exercises — domain detection drills, differential diagnosis practice, calibration against expert reads
  • Counter-narrative training — practice catching yourself defaulting to personal narratives instead of structural reads
  • Fully worked case walkthroughs — 5-8 per course, showing the complete diagnostic process from raw situation to structural read
  • Structured submission prompts — designed to produce real structural work, not therapy-flavored summaries
  • Expert review — detailed structural critique of your case submissions, not just pass/fail
