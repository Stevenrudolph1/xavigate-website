The Courses

The Foundation: Diagnostic Seeing

Can this person see past the story people tell to what's actually going on?

You learn why capable people deplete in situations that should work — and why everyone's first instinct (to look at the person) is usually wrong. By the end, you can tell whether the real issue is how the work is set up, how well it fits the person, or how someone is showing up in the room.

Completion

Each course ends with case submissions — real situations analyzed using what you learned. You receive detailed feedback on your work, and cases that show you can see past the story to the real issue earn completion. One revision is included; a second is available for a $100 resubmission fee.

“Completed Renergence for Practitioners Training — 2026”

Year-stamped. It signals recency — when you did the work — not rank.

Completion means you can reference the training in your professional materials, say you're trained in the Renergence framework, and describe that you use Renergence diagnosis in your work. Completers can also opt in to a public directory — a factual list of people who did the work.

Already Know Multiple Natures?

MN lives inside the Renergence framework — it's the part about what naturally engages people. If you already know MN, your skills transfer directly — you're further along than you think.

Multiple Natures Revisited is a short, free book that explains how MN fits into the bigger picture. It connects what you already know to the full Renergence framework and shows where Structure and Positioning go beyond what MN covers.

Everyone starts at the Foundation — the diagnostic seeing course is new even for experienced MN practitioners. But the Alignment course will feel like home.

If you want to use MNTEST professionally, the path is: Foundation → Alignment → MNTEST.

If you hold an existing MN Practitioner certification, your credential stands. Contact us about how it relates to RPT.
