Renergence for Practitioners

When something isn't working — and you can't tell why — the problem isn't your skill. It's that you're diagnosing in the dark. This training gives you a clean way to locate where a problem actually lives, before you try to fix it.

You've been in this situation. A client is stuck. You try one angle. It helps a little. Then it fades. You try a different framing. A different tool. Still not quite it.

The problem isn't that you lack skill. It's that everything blends together.

Without a diagnostic frame, exhaustion looks like a mindset issue. Misfit looks like burnout. Structural overload looks like weak boundaries. Engagement collapse looks like a personality problem. So you compensate by adding more tools. More frameworks. More certifications. More methods.

But adding tools doesn't solve mislocation. Diagnosis does.

What Changes When You Can Diagnose

Instead of: "Let's work on your boundaries."
You can see: This isn't a boundary issue. The load is mislocated — something is missing in how the work is set up.

Instead of: "Maybe you're in the wrong career."
You can see: The career may be fine. The arrangement is fighting the person's natural pattern of engagement.

Instead of: "I need to be more present with clients."
You can see: I'm not failing at presence. I'm stuck in one way of engaging and can't shift.

Clarity reduces effort. Precision replaces guessing. You stop stacking interventions and start working where the problem actually is.

Who This Is For

You already work with people in complex situations. You're the one in the room trying to figure out what's actually happening underneath. This training sharpens that read.

  • Coaches and executive coaches who sense the issue is deeper than what the client is saying
  • HR and OD professionals who keep solving the same problem in different forms
  • Managers and team leads who see good people deplete in roles that should work
  • Therapists and counselors who see workplace distress that isn't clinical
  • Consultants who advise on people, teams, or organizations and need to diagnose before they prescribe

You don't need a specific credential. You don't need another toolbox. You need a cleaner map.

The Training Pathway

You learn the diagnostic model first. Then you go deep in the area where your work lives. The pathway is modular — take one course or build full capability over time.

Step 1: Foundations in Renergence — learn to separate the three places problems live

Step 2: Choose a Focus Track — go deep in Structure, Alignment, or Positioning

Step 3 (optional): MNTEST — learn to measure engagement fit with an assessment tool

Most practitioners start with Foundation + one Focus Track. You can stop at any point. Every course stands on its own.

By the end, you've built a body of practiced analysis that shows how you think.

The Courses

Foundations in Renergence

Where is the problem actually coming from — and how do you tell?

Someone is struggling and everyone has a theory about what's wrong with them. Sometimes it's obvious—they're burned out. Sometimes they're performing fine but something's off. Sometimes it's just… flat. This course teaches you to stop looking at the person and start looking at the situation. You learn the three places problems actually live—and how to tell which one is driving what you're seeing.

After this course: You can walk into a situation where someone is struggling and identify whether the real issue is the work setup, the fit, or how someone is showing up — instead of defaulting to "what's wrong with them."
