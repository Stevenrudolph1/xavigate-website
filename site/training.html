Renergence for Practitioners

Learn to see what's actually going wrong in a situation — not the story people tell about it. Whether someone is burned out, performing fine but off, or just quietly flat — there's usually a condition underneath that no one is looking at. This training teaches you to find it.

Who This Is For

You work with people. Someone is struggling — burned out, stuck, quietly flat — and you're the one in the room trying to figure out what's actually going on. You've tried the obvious moves: coaching on boundaries, resilience conversations, mindset shifts. Sometimes they help. Often they don't hold. That's because they're aimed at the person — and the problem is usually somewhere else.

This training is for practitioners who want to stop guessing and start seeing the structural cause. That includes:

  • Coaches who sense the issue is deeper than what the client is saying — and want a framework for finding it
  • HR and OD professionals who keep solving the same problems because the real cause hasn't been addressed
  • Managers and team leads who want to understand why good people deplete in roles that should work
  • Therapists and counselors who see workplace distress that isn't clinical — and want a structural read, not just a clinical one
  • Consultants who advise on people, teams, or organizations and want to diagnose before they prescribe

You don't need a specific credential to start. You need to work with people and want a sharper read on what's actually happening.

How the Training Works

Start with the foundation, then go deep in the area where your work lives.

Build a Learning Portfolio

Most courses hand you content and check whether you absorbed it. This one asks you to use it — on real situations, from the beginning. You build a Learning Portfolio: a set of documents where you do your analysis, work through exercises, and develop your own case work. By the end, you haven't just studied the framework. You've built a body of practiced analysis that shows how you think.

Everything Is Yours to Keep

No platform. No login. No software that expires. When you enroll, you receive the complete course materials — manual, exercises, case walkthroughs, submission prompts. They're yours permanently. Only the submission window has a deadline.

How Review Works

When you submit your portfolio, Steven Rudolph reviews your work directly inside your documents — inline comments on where your analysis landed, where it drifted, and what a sharper read would look like. You also receive a final review report. It's not a grade. It's a detailed, honest read of how you're seeing.

Two Tracks, Same Standard

Every course is available in two tracks. Self-Paced gives you the complete materials, your Learning Portfolio, and expert review. With Live Instructor adds up to 2.5 hours of 1-on-1 time via Zoom — working through your actual cases, getting real-time feedback, and calibrating your reads against expert analysis.

The Courses

Foundations in Renergence

Where is the problem actually coming from — and how do you tell?

Someone is struggling and everyone has a theory about what's wrong with them. Sometimes it's obvious—they're burned out. Sometimes they're performing fine but something's off. Sometimes it's just… flat. This course teaches you to stop looking at the person and start looking at the situation. You learn the three places problems actually live—and how to tell which one is driving what you're seeing.

After this course: You can walk into a situation where someone is struggling and identify whether the real issue is the work setup, the fit, or how someone is showing up — instead of defaulting to "what's wrong with them."

Structure: Finding What's Missing

When people carry too much because something is missing in how the work is set up. Learn to find the absence, name it, and design the fix so the situation holds without someone heroically filling the gap.

You'll be able to: Audit what people are carrying, find what's structurally missing, and design changes to the system — not the person.

Alignment: When the Work Doesn't Fit

When the work doesn't fit who someone is — even when they're succeeding at it. The role demands one thing, but what naturally engages the person is something else. Learn to see the mismatch and name what's actually off.

You'll be able to: Spot when success is hiding depletion, surface the cost in conversation, and help someone see what actually fits.

Positioning: How You Show Up

How practitioners get stuck in one way of engaging — helping when they should step back, analyzing when they should act, withdrawing when they should stay in it. Learn to notice the pattern in real time and move deliberately.

You'll be able to: Catch your own default pattern mid-session and choose a different way to show up — instead of running on autopilot.
