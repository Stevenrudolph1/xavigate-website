What Renergence Is

When what you're in costs more than it returns—sometimes obviously, sometimes invisibly—that's the signal. It could be the arrangement itself: how load, decisions, and responsibilities are distributed. It could be fit: whether the work matches how you naturally engage. Or it could be where you're standing inside the situation—what you see from there, and what you miss. The books name what's operating. The case review diagnoses the specific loop.
