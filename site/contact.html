Note on Business Model

The business focuses on one-to-many products (books, courses, tools, platforms) rather than one-to-one consulting or implementation services. This ensures the work can scale without requiring proportional increases in time, and maintains coherence with the governance principles that guide the work.

If you're interested in consulting or custom implementation, we can connect you with practitioners who work independently using these frameworks.
