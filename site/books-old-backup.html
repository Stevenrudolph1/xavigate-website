How These Areas Relate

All four address the same underlying reality: people operate inside systems, systems are structured, and structure shapes what becomes possible. Each area provides a different lens on that relationship.

Renergence reveals whether something returns more than it takes, over time.

Structure prescribes structural moves when heroics become normal.

Natures clarifies how people differ in what energizes or drains them.

Engagement shows where practitioners stand and what becomes available from each position.
